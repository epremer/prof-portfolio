About Me

Hi there! I'm a student of the Computer Science post-baccalaureate program at Oregon State University. I'm originally from the beautiful Central Coast of California. When I first started my journey as a computer scientist, I had been successfully running a thriving small massage therapy practice which I sold in June 2018 to pursue my studies full-time.

I am currently seeking internships for Summer/Fall 2019 and/or Winter 2020. My current goal is to learn as much as possible about computer operating systems and system administration, Site Reliability Engineering, and web development using NodeJs and ReactJS.

I love to make useful services and products. When I'm not studying or working on side projects, I can be found hiking, backpacking, playing my favourite board games, or building elaborate 3D cakes.

Aptitudes

Foresight

An aptitude for seeing possibilities, foresight can help one determine the best appraoch to goal setting. This is "bigger-picture" thinking.

According to the Johnson O'Connor test, I scored in the 99th percentile, which means I tend to have a sense of vision that can help me see beyond obstacles to complete objectives.

Ideaphoria

Ideaphoria is the rate of flow of ideas, thoughts, plans, and projects.

According to the Johnson O'Connor test, I scored in the 95th percentile, which means I can quickly form ideas and thoughts. Although they may not always be the best ideas, with my high flow of ideas, I'm bound to come up with a few keepers.

Memory for Design

According to the Johnson O'Connor test, Memory for Design is the ability to remember designs and can be used for constructing three-dimensional structures. I scored 95th percentile in this area.

I love working with my hands; taking things apart, making 3D prototypes of my ideas, etc. I really feel alive when constructive an idea from scratch, and the structural problem solving that goes into its construction.

Inductive Reasoning

Inductive Reasoning is the ability to quickly see a common element among heterogeneous ideas, observations, or facts. It is the forming of theories that fit experimental evidence.

Structural Visualization

Structural Visualization is the ability to visualize the structure of a three-dimensional form.

I use this aptitude especially when planning what my next cake creation will look like, but it also helps when I get an assignment specification to visualize how the assignment should look and feel.
