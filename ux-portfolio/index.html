Lizz Learns UX Design

Description
I have a huge passion for design, and efficiency. UX Design allows me to make a website beautiful and efficient. This particular website is still under construction, as I'm getting a feel for how to actually implement my designs. Hopefully you can grasp the intention behind my designs and the passion with which I approach various solutions.
The Problem
I am currently in a Computer Science program at Oregon State University, and took a Usability Engineering course, which I LOVED! Unfortanately, that is the only Usability course available to me as an undergraduate student.
The Solution
My solution has been to shadow the UX department of my most recent Software Engineering internship, so I can learn that company's UX processes.

Another solution is to complete the User Experience Design course at General Assembly in September 2019. I will learn UX elements of design, research, wireframing tools and techniques, low- and high-fidelity prototyping, and design presentation. I'm eager to take on this weeklong intensive accelerated course and improve my natural talents.
