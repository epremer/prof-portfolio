I Received a Scholarship for OSU!
28 April 2018
The Low-Down
In late March, I was awarded the Randy V. Puckett Memorial Scholarship by CBT Nuggets! How exciting is that?!

I've been applying to FAFSA and OSU Scholarships and any scholarships that I seem even remotely eligible for, but, frankly, I did not expect to receive any awards short of loans.
The Deets
Not only was this a huge honour, but I also had the opportunity to virtually meet a few of the representatives who knew Randy Puckett when he worked at CBT Nuggets.

I feel oddly drawn to this man. From the limited information I could find on the internet, he embodied the CBT Nuggets core values; respect for people and their time, he was friendly, had a genuine passion for what he did, and had an emphasis on continual improvement. In my video chat with the representatives, I'm told he was honest, humble, kind, creative, and compassionate.

Strangely enough, what first drew me to him in the initial research stages, is that he was born two days before my mom was born and died two days before my mom died. I'm not particularly superstitious or anything, I just thought it was a nice little reminder of how closely anything can remind us of the people we love, who have died, and who loved us.
