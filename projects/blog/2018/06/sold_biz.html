After Six Years, I Sold My Business!
30 May 2018



Edna Valley Massage is no more...
In 2012, I started my small massage practice, Edna Valley Massage - then Massage Therapy by Lizz Premer. I built it into a thriving, 5-star practice complete with Ashiatsu Deepfeet Bar Therapy - which you should try if you LOVE Deep Tissue Massages and ever have the opportunity (there are a lot of Ashiatsu therapists in the Portland area).

I love giving people massages and helping them feel better. But it's not terribly intellectually stimulating - not at all like Computer Science has proven itself to be!!
What Now?
Well, as of June 1, 2018, I will no longer be a small business owner. :D Now I'm a full-time student. I can focus fully on my studies, instead of also having the responsibilities of a full-time business owner with two independent contractors and over 350 clients. ... Yes, I said I was thriving. But as difficult a decision as it was, it was time - maybe even later than I should have "gotten out". Meh, everything in its own timing.
