Another Scholarship!... for Travel!
14 May 2018
What Is the What?
WOW! I can't believe it. I got a travel scholarship to go to one of OSU's bi-annual post-baccalaurete career showcases!

I absolutely would not have even considered going had the school not awarded me with the much-needed moolah to get there. And it's just the right amount, too. Enough to drive up in a rental - a 16 hour drive for me - stay at the nice hotel it's being held for one night - the night before my 29th birthday! - and a little bit for gas. My godmom has a brother in Portland area whose wife is very excited I can stay with them. I'm excited too!
When Is It?
The Career Showcase is June 18, 2018 in Tigard, Oregon.
Who Will Be There?
I'm sure I'll have another opportunity to write about my experiences once I've gone to the showcase, but it sounds like some great companies will be there, presenting and recruiting. And some of my professors will be there, too, something I'm very excited about.
Preparation
This website is actually one way I'm preparing for the showcase. It'll be a nice way to show off a bit of my skills and interests.
